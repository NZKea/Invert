Invert is a new, unique wooden sign product that offers both personalised and pre-made purchasing options.

Each Invert uses a puzzle-like design to decode words so that they can only be seen by 'reading between the lines'. Primarily featured in a traditional black and white colour, each product comes equipped with wooden stand legs so that it can be easily displayed in any home, bedroom, or desk space. Fresh and attention-grabbing, our product brings fun and creativity to all our customers and their families and friends.
